## How to pull the Pasco docket yourself (~2 minutes, free)

1. Go to `pascoclerk.com` → Records → **Search Court Records** → "Online Court Records Search" (Civitek OCRS, Pasco = county 51).
2. Click **Public** → **Accept** the disclaimer.
3. **Case Search** tab → Year: `2026`, Court Type: `County Civil (CC)`, Sequence #: `001256` → check the "I am human" box → Search.
4. Open the case → full docket history, court events, and document images. Anything view-restricted online can be requested from the Dade City clerk's office, records line (352) 521-4542.
